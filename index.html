Muscle Index Dividend & Tax

🎯 Mission — Solving 3 Major Issues

  • Reducing healthcare costs — reward preventive health; reduce chronic disease burden
  • Reducing wealth & income gap — dividend to healthy citizens; tax multipliers on the less healthy
  • Staying competitive in the AGI age — health becomes the new non-automatable scarce asset

🌟 Vision — Replace Wealth with Health

In a world where robots produce abundance, the only thing that cannot be automated, inherited, or bought is the discipline to build and maintain a healthy body.

Why Skeletal Muscle Mass Index (SMMI)?

  • Cannot be inherited, bought, or surgically implanted
  • Builds only through years of consistent resistance training
  • Degrades rapidly without sustained effort — always reflects current self
  • Predicts longevity, metabolic health, and fall prevention
❌ Not VO₂ max — formula biased toward runners; not directly measured
❌ Not fat % alone — used as a limit filter only; athletes naturally fall below normal ranges
