Why Skeletal Muscle Mass Index?

SMMI = Skeletal Muscle Mass (kg) ÷ Height² (m²). The only health asset that cannot be inherited, bought, or surgically implanted.
🚫Cannot be inherited
💊Cannot be surgically added
⏳Degrades without effort
🧬Predicts longevity
❌ Not VO₂ max — formula biased toward runners, not directly measured
❌ Fat % used as upper limit only — no lower bound; athletes naturally fall below
